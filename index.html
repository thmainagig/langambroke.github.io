Ugali Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  1. A mug of water
  2. A packet of flour
  3. A sufuria
  4. Source of heat
  5. A wooden mwiko
  6. Optional(BlueBand)

PREPARATION

  • Boil two cups of water in a sufuria
  • Put some flour and stir
  • Bake it at a little temperature

SERVING

  • Serve with steamed vegetables
  • Can also serve with well cooked meat
